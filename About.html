ABOUT US

Innovating Structural Design for a Safer, Stronger Future

Eiffel Industries, LLC is a premier structural engineering and design firm located in Scottsdale, AZ, with over two decades of pioneering experience in the industry. Founded by Sam Biswas, PE, our company is driven by a commitment to delivering innovative, cost-effective engineering solutions while maintaining the highest standards of safety and performance.

We pride ourselves on our ability to navigate complex building codes and regulations with ease, ensuring that every project proceeds smoothly from concept to completion.

We serve the Phoenix Metropolitan Area and beyond, working on a wide array of projects that include everything from residential single-family homes and multifamily developments to large-scale commercial, industrial, educational, and healthcare facilities.

OUR STORY

A Legacy of Excellence, Built on Innovation and Integrity

Eiffel Industries, LLC was founded with a vision to transform the way structural engineering is approached. Established by Sam Biswas, PE, with over 20 years of hands-on experience, the company has grown from a small, passionate team into one of the most trusted names in the Phoenix Metropolitan Area.

From day one, our goal has been to deliver engineering solutions that not only meet but exceed the expectations of our clients.

Our journey began with a focus on residential projects, but as we honed our craft and expanded our expertise, we evolved into a full-service structural engineering firm. Today, we handle everything from multifamily developments and corporate offices to hospitals, schools, and industrial complexes.

What has remained constant throughout our growth is our unwavering commitment to precision, quality, and client satisfaction.

At the core of our story is a belief in the power of collaboration. We understand that every project is unique, and by working closely with architects, contractors, and property owners, we ensure that each structure is as functional as it is innovative.

OUR SERVICES

Comprehensive Structural Engineering Services

Residential Homes

At Eiffel Industries, LLC, we specialize in designing and engineering residential homes that prioritize safety, comfort, and modern living. Whether it’s a single-family home or a multifamily complex, our focus is on creating structures that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also built to last. We incorporate sustainable materials and energy-efficient solutions to ensure that homes remain eco-friendly and cost-effective for homeowners. Our team works closely with developers and homeowners to bring their vision to life, ensuring that every space is functional, inviting, and tailored to modern living needs.

Healthcare Structures

We bring specialized expertise to healthcare projects, designing hospitals, clinics, and medical facilities that adhere to stringent safety and regulatory standards. At Eiffel Industries, LLC, we understand the unique needs of healthcare environments, from patient accessibility to efficient workflows for medical staff. Our designs focus on creating safe, sterile, and comfortable spaces that promote healing and ensure operational efficiency. Each project is approached with precision and attention to detail, ensuring that the facilities meet both immediate healthcare needs and long-term adaptability.

Educational Facilities

At Eiffel Industries, LLC, we understand the unique challenges and requirements that come with designing Educational Facilities. From schools and universities to student housing, our team is dedicated to creating safe, functional, and inspiring spaces where students and educators can thrive. We ensure that every project meets strict safety standards, while also integrating sustainable practices to build eco-friendly environments conducive to learning. Our designs focus on durability and adaptability, ensuring that the buildings can evolve with changing educational needs. Whether it's designing classrooms, libraries, laboratories, or dormitories, we work closely with institutions to develop efficient, cost-effective structures that foster creativity, collaboration, and growth.

Industrial Buildings

Eiffel Industries, LLC offers tailored engineering solutions for industrial facilities, including warehouses, factories, and manufacturing plants. Our designs emphasize efficiency, durability, and scalability, allowing industrial operations to run smoothly while minimizing operational costs. We ensure that every industrial building is built to withstand heavy equipment, high-traffic areas, and the demands of large-scale production. With a focus on cost-effective construction methods and compliance with industry standards, we help our clients achieve their operational goals with structures built for performance and longevity.

Commercial Projects

Eiffel Industries, LLC provides comprehensive structural engineering services for commercial projects ranging from corporate offices to small businesses. We understand that commercial spaces need to be versatile, cost-efficient, and designed for growth. Our team focuses on creating durable and flexible structures that can adapt to the changing needs of businesses while ensuring compliance with all safety and building codes. Whether it's designing retail spaces or office complexes, we deliver innovative solutions that enhance both functionality and aesthetic appeal.

Renovations & Retrofits

Our renovation and retrofitting services at Eiffel Industries, LLC are designed to modernize existing structures while improving safety and functionality. Whether it's updating older buildings to meet current codes or enhancing their efficiency, we approach each project with a focus on preserving the structure’s integrity while integrating new technologies and design innovations. We work closely with property owners and contractors to ensure that every renovation is seamless, cost-effective, and adds long-term value to the building.
