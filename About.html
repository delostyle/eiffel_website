ABOUT US

Innovating Structural Design for a Safer, Stronger Future

Eiffel Industries, LLC is a premier structural engineering and design firm located in Scottsdale, AZ, with over two decades of pioneering experience in the industry. Founded by Sam Biswas, PE, our company is driven by a commitment to delivering innovative, cost-effective engineering solutions while maintaining the highest standards of safety and performance.

We pride ourselves on our ability to navigate complex building codes and regulations with ease, ensuring that every project proceeds smoothly from concept to completion.

We serve the Phoenix Metropolitan Area and beyond, working on a wide array of projects that include everything from residential single-family homes and multifamily developments to large-scale commercial, industrial, educational, and healthcare facilities.

OUR STORY

A Legacy of Excellence, Built on Innovation and Integrity

Eiffel Industries, LLC was founded with a vision to transform the way structural engineering is approached. Established by Sam Biswas, PE, with over 20 years of hands-on experience, the company has grown from a small, passionate team into one of the most trusted names in the Phoenix Metropolitan Area.

From day one, our goal has been to deliver engineering solutions that not only meet but exceed the expectations of our clients.

Our journey began with a focus on residential projects, but as we honed our craft and expanded our expertise, we evolved into a full-service structural engineering firm. Today, we handle everything from multifamily developments and corporate offices to hospitals, schools, and industrial complexes.

What has remained constant throughout our growth is our unwavering commitment to precision, quality, and client satisfaction.

At the core of our story is a belief in the power of collaboration. We understand that every project is unique, and by working closely with architects, contractors, and property owners, we ensure that each structure is as functional as it is innovative.
